Passionate About Books

Stories in books create a wonderful adventure for many people, that develops into a passion. Those stories makes them continue reading more books and become passionate about books, or reading them!

Books open minds, giving the reader or audience something to think about! This is due to the author writing from a historical standpoint. It stems from magnificent statements or historical figures who have had statements of impact.

Intro to Love of Books

There are many talented authors who can project words of unimaginable strength onto a page. Once someone reads their book, it turns to an wonderful experience. I've had the pleasure of reading one book called Stalin by the author Stephen Kotkin.


Although a notorious figure in history, the author has spun a web of words that got me caught into reading his scholastic book. I still am trying to read it during free time. There are other books that have interested me with talented authors. And it is in reading these books that inspires me to wanna become a great writer. It is a monumental achievement that they wrote these books and I'm still trying to read the charismatic words printed on the pages.


Like this book, there are plenty of books that has wonderful stories trapped into the pages for many to read. This is one of the reasons that many become passionate about books. Why many visit libraries and wait the next selection of great books to be devoured by the eyes; like a feast by a great chef.

It is like a James Bond or Jason Bourne film. reading is a fun mission to be handled greatly. Once the mission is completed, the books wait on the next person to read them. It is such an adventure. But it takes a talented writer to catch those words onto the pages.
